Tanki Online - Efficiency Rating Guide

This guide explains how the efficiency system works and outlines the optimal way to compete for a Top 100 efficiency rating.

What Is Efficiency?

According to the official Tanki Wiki, efficiency (also called effectiveness) is calculated using a non-disclosed formula that reflects how effective a player is in battles. A separate leaderboard tracks the Top 100 players by efficiency.

Rank Bonuses

From Rank 1 to Rank 31 (Legend), players gain a flat 3,000 efficiency points per rank as long as the following conditions are met:

  • Minimum 2.0 Kill/Death ratio
  • Crystal/Experience (C/E) ratio of 1.0 or higher

If your C/E ratio drops below 1.0, the bonus is reduced (typically 2,500-2,800). After Rank 31, the flat rank bonus no longer applies.

Optimal Modes and Maps

The most efficient gameplay happens on large maps with many players, maximizing battle activity and battle fund.

  • Siege - 50 battle score for 15 experience
  • Rugby - 80 battle score for 30 experience (up to 7 deliveries)
  • Capture the Flag - Similar to Rugby, capped at 5 flags

Why the Leaderboard Barely Changes And Why It's Changing Now

The Top 100 list has remained mostly unchanged since 2021 due to experience inflation we used to have. This was intentional so you would rank up faster and have weaker equipment. But it changed thanks to these recent patches:

  • Patch #794 (March 28, 2025) - Experience gains reduced by 25%.
  • Patch #800 (May 27, 2025) - Premium Pass crystal bonus increased from +100% to +150%.

Minimum Requirements for Top 100

  • 100% Premium Pass uptime
  • Mode priority: Siege > Rugby > CTF
  • 2.0+ Kill/Death ratio
  • 2.2+ Crystal/Experience ratio
  • Large maps with high player counts
  • Ignore Gold Boxes at all costs, except player dropped ones
